Oaxaca Airport to Town
The center of Oaxaca is about 20 minutes from the airport. Once you land and pass customs, head to the "Taxi TIcket" booth in the aiport, next to the car rental agencies. Buy a collectivo ticket for zone 1 for $70 MXN and go outside, where you will be assigned to a van. Alternatively, if traveling with a group heading to the same location, it is more cost effective to rent an especial van. This is $285 MXN for up to 4 people of $410 MXN for up to 8. There are no cash exhange places in the Oaxaca airport but there are 3 ATMs. These offer the best exchange rates. The airport is very small and easy to navigate!
Flights to the Beach
We know traveling to Oaxaca takes some time, and hope that our guests can take this opportunity to make a full trip out of it. Oaxaca state is home to some of the best beaches in Mexico. They are about 6 hours by land, or :30 minutes by air. Aero Tucan offers daily flights.
Puerto Escondido
A bohemian surf town popular with Australians and Europeans (particularly Italians), Puerto Escondido's main draw is the world class surf beach of Zicatela (pros only), but also has several bays for surfers or trippers of all skill levels. Make freshly caught shrimp cocktail and whole red snapper your every day meals!
