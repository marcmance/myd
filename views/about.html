about us

Maharlika and Damian first hit it off in the fifth ward of New Brunswick, orginizing concerts and films for the RU crowd. They discovered a shared passion for long walks along the river, rock and roll concerts, haircuts in the moonlight and travels into the unknown.

It was one of these treks across the colonial and mesoamerican sites of Mexico that first brought them to the Zapotec valley of Oaxaca. After a short hiatus, they hit it off once again in Jersey City and Montclair, nurturing their shared humble upbringings, and cementing what is to be a life long romance.

Together, they select the culturally diverse city of Oaxaca as the venue for their holy matrimony.
