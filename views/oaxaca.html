about oaxaca

Located in the south of Mexico and including a long Pacific coast, Oaxaca state has long been neglected by federal authorities but cradled some of the finest thinkers and movements that built the Nation. Oaxaca state has its main roots with the Zapotec and Mixtec civilization, with many diverse languages and cultures across its mountain ranges and valleys of the Sierra Madre Occidental.

Thanks to its colonial architecture and rich flavours of a staple cousine that includes mole, chocolate, coffee and cheese, Oaxaca City (officially named Oaxaca de Juarez) has been a travel hub for European and Australian travelers for many decades. A decidedly independent and strong political stand has created autonomous municipalities around its mountain range, as well as bohemian beach towns along its shore lines.
