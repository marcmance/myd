Accommondations

There are many beautiful options in Oaxaca. We hand selected the following places (listed in order of price) for our guests. Please call or email and mention "La Boda de Maharlika y Damián".

Hotel La Casona de Tita

Small but well-appointed hotel with large rooms surrounding a private courtyard. Five rooms available.

Palacio Borghese

Luxorious rooms, albeit with ornate, out of context interior design. Rooftop with great views of Santo Domingo. Eight rooms available.

Casa Catrina

Beautiful hotel with design that enhances colonial bones. Six rooms available, book one of the suites with private large terraces!

La Catrina de Alcala

Boutique hotel and gallery right on the main pedestrian mall. Thirteen rooms available.

Casa de Siete Balcones

Cozy and centrally located with warm and welcoming management. Seven rooms available.

Hostal de La Noria

Spacious hacienda with multiple outdoor spaces located across the street from the reception venue. Fourteen rooms available.
