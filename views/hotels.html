Accommodations

There are many beautiful options in Oaxaca. We hand selected the following places (listed in order of price) for our guests. Please call or email and mention "la boda de Maharlika y Damián".

Hotel La Casona de Tita

Small but well-appointed hotel with large rooms surrounding a private courtyard. Five rooms available.

Palacio Borghese

Luxorious rooms, albeit with ornate, out of context interior design. Rooftop with great views of Santo Domingo. Eight rooms available.

Casa Catrina

Beautiful hotel with design that enhances colonial bones. Six rooms available, book one of the suites with private large terraces!

La Catrina de Alcala

Boutique hotel and gallery right on the main pedestrian mall. Thirteen rooms available.

Casa de Siete Balcones

Cozy and centrally located with warm and welcoming management. Seven rooms available.

Hostal de La Noria

Spacious hacienda with multiple outdoor spaces located across the street from the reception venue. Fourteen rooms available.

El Secreto

Private guest house with hip artisan decor and great breakfast spreads. Three rooms available, great for a group.

Casa de los Milagros

Large private colonial home with comfortable amenitites. Three rooms available, great for a group.

Diablo y Sandia (Boca del Monte)

The new second location of this popular bed and breakfast with fresh artful Oaxacan details throughout and large common areas. Eight rooms available.

We do not have blocks at these hotels, but they are additional options.

Quinta Real

Beautifully converted 16th century convent with lush grounds and luxorious colonial details. Availability to potentially open up in February.

Hotel Azul

Another boutique hotel and gallery with modern design and style. Availability to potentially open up in February.

Hotel Abu

What this hotel lacks in traditional charm, it makes up for in location and value. Clean rooms and contemporary design.

Hotel CasAntica

Newly renovated hotel combining colonial achitecture with modern comforts.
