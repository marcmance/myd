Hotel

We have a hotel block at Quinta Real, but there are many beautiful options in Oaxaca. Below are some suggestions.

Quinta Real

Beautifully converted 16th century convent with lush grounds and and luxorious colonial details.

Hotel Azul

Boutique hotel and gallery with modern design and style.

Casa de Siete Balcones

Cozy and centrally located with warm and welcoming management.

Hotel La Casona De Tita

Small but fancy and well-appointed hotel with large rooms surrounding a private courtyard.

Hotel Oaxaca Real

Mid-range option across the street from where the bride and groom are staying.

El Diablo y la Sandia

Hip little spot in the middle of town with five affordable rooms, communal kitchen and fridge, fresh styling all around
