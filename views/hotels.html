Accommodations

There are many beautiful options in Oaxaca. We hand selected the following places for our guests. Please call or email and mention "la boda de Maharlika y Damián" to get the discounted rates. Our top picks are listed first!

Casa Catrina

Beautiful hotel with design that enhances colonial bones. Six rooms available, book one of the suites with private large terraces! ($1600 MXN - $2100 MXN)

La Catrina de Alcala

Boutique hotel and gallery right on the main pedestrian mall. Thirteen rooms available. ($1500 MXN - $1700 MXN)

El Secreto

Private guest house with hip artisan decor and great breakfast spreads. Three rooms available, great for a group. ($1200 MXN - $1500 MXN)

Hotel La Casona de Tita

Small but well-appointed hotel with large rooms surrounding a private courtyard. Five rooms available. ($3050 MXN - $3950 MXN)

Diablo y Sandia (Boca del Monte)

The new second location of this popular bed and breakfast with fresh artful Oaxacan details throughout and large common areas. Eight rooms available. ($900 MXN - $1500 MXN)

Casa de los Milagros

Large private colonial home with comfortable amenitites. Three rooms available, great for a group. ($1300 MXN - $1500 MXN)

Casa de Siete Balcones

Cozy and centrally located with warm and welcoming management. Seven rooms available. ($1450 MXN - $1550 MXN)

Hostal de La Noria

Spacious hacienda with multiple outdoor spaces located across the street from the reception venue. Fourteen rooms available. ($1400 MXN - $2100 MXN)

Palacio Borghese

Luxorious rooms, albeit with ornate, out of context interior design. Rooftop with great views of Santo Domingo. Eight rooms available. ($2800 MXN - $3300 MXN)

We do not have blocks at these hotels, but they are additional options.
